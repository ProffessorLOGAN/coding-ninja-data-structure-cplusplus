Number Pattern 2

Print the following pattern
Pattern for N = 4

. . . 1
. . 2 3
. 3 4 5
4 5 6 7

The dots represent spaces.



Input Format :
N (Total no. of rows)4

Output Format :
Pattern in N lines
Sample Input :

5
Sample Output :

. . . . 1
. . . 2 3
. . 3 4 5
. 4 5 6 7
5 6 7 8 9

The dots represent spaces.
